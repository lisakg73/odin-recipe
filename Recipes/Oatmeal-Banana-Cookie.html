Oatmeal-Banana-Cookie

Description

Simple, easy to make cookie.

Ingredients

  • 3 bananas, mashed
  • 1/3 cup applesauce
  • 2 cups oats
  • 3 tablespoons milk
  • 1 tablesppon vanilla
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven 350 F.
  2. Combine all ingredients and mix well.
  3. Spoon cookie dough onto a greased baking sheet and bake for 15-20 minutes until done.
