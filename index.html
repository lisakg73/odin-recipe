Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is an Italian recipe made with noodles, meat, cheese, sauce, and spices. Simple and easy to make. This is one dish that is enjoyed by many and has many different ways to make it.

Ingredients

  • noodles
  • meat
  • cheese
  • sauce
  • spices

Steps

  1. Boil noodles until stick to a wall (al dente)
  2. Cook meat and spices in frying pan until done.
  3. Drain noodles to remove all water.
  4. Layer noodles on bottom of a casserole dish.
  5. Layer meat on top of noodles.
  6. Layer sauce and then cheese.
  7. Repeat each layer until three layers have been completed with cheese being the final topping.
  8. Place in oven on 350 F until for 20 minutes to allow cheese to melt in each layer.
